Innovative Audio Tools from Istanbul

Soundtrackistanbul is a company established to create tools in the field of audio programming. As someone who lives in Istanbul and draws inspiration from the city's soundscape, I use this platform to share the plugins I develop with the help of artificial intelligence.

I work with the JUCE framework and C++ to design performance-oriented audio instruments. These tools are optimized for ARM-based systems and aim to use minimal CPU and memory while delivering high efficiency. As a composer actively involved in music for games, films, and production libraries, I focus on creating practical and useful instruments for real-world scenarios.

About

Soundtrackistanbul was founded to explore new possibilities in the world of audio programming and plugin development. Based in Istanbul, I find endless inspiration in the rhythms and textures of this city. My goal is to turn those impressions into sound tools that are both creative and technically solid.

The journey of Soundtrackistanbul began as a personal exploration into combining music, code, and technology in meaningful ways. With a background in both composition and design, I bring a practical perspective to my development process. I use the JUCE framework and C++ to build audio plugins and instruments that are efficient and accessible.

These tools are optimized for modern systems including Apple's ARM-based chips, and designed to be as light as possible on system resources without compromising on sound quality. Each plugin is created with the mindset of a composer and sound designer who understands real-world needs.

As someone who has composed music for games, films, and production libraries, I aim to build instruments that I would personally want to use in my own projects. Soundtrackistanbul is a place where creativity meets functionality, and I hope that these tools will help other musicians and creators bring their ideas to life.

Thank you for being here, and I hope you find something that inspires you.
